Geography



North Carolina is known for having the Great Smoky Mountains, otherwise known as
"The Smokies". Their largest mountain is Mount Mitchell, which is actually deemed to ones of the
largest mountains on the Eastern side of the country.It has a Coastal Plain covering 45% of the state
and has hundreds of lakes like Lake Waccamaw, largest of the bays. The climate there is mild and
equiable, being closer to the equator than a lot of states.
