Government



The government is similar to that of all American states. Although, there are some abnormal laws that North Carolina has put into effect.



  1. It is illegal to sing off key.
  2. Serving alcohol at a bingo game is not allowed.
  3. You can't steal more than $1,000 of grease.
  4. Elephants cannot be used to plow cotton fields.
  5. Bingo games may not last over 5 hours unless it's held at a fair.
  6. Local planning agencies’ cannot use climate change science to predict sea-level rise.
  7. All couples staying overnight in a hotel must have a room with double beds that are at least two feet apart.
  8. A marriage can be declared void if either of the two persons is physically impotent.
  9. In Rocky Mount, North Carolina; it is required that you must pay a property tax on your dog.
  10. In Charlotte, North Carolina; women must have their bodies covered by at least 16 yards of cloth at all times.
