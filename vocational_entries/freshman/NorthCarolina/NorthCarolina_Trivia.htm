Trivia About North Carolina


  1. The first drawbridge built in the United States was built over the Cape Fear River.
  2. The world's smallest daily newspaper is the Tryon Daily Bulletin in North Carolina.
  3. Jockey's Ridge is the tallest natural sand dune system in the Eastern United States. Its height ranges from 80 to 100 feet.
  4. The first Pepsi was created and served in New Bern.
  5. Albemarle Sound is the largest freshwater sound in the world.
  6. The first known miniature golf course was in Fayetteville, North Carolina.
  7. Fontana Dam is the tallest dam in the Eastern United States.
  8. North Carolina was the first state to declare independence from England with the Mecklenburg Declaration of 1775.
  9. Grandfather Mountain is home to the nation's highest swinging bridge-one mile high.
  10. The Biltmore Estate in Asheville is the largest private house in the world, with 250 rooms.
